# Ridge Gourd Dal Kuzhambu with Kodo Millet

## Summary
A balanced South Indian lunch bowl where legumes, vegetables, and whole grains carry the dish instead of excess fat. Ridge Gourd Dal Kuzhambu with Kodo Millet brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Kodo Millet]], washed
- 90 g [[Toor Dal]], washed
- 180 g [[Ridge Gourd]], chopped
- 60 g [[Onion]], chopped
- 90 g [[Tomato]], chopped
- 10 g [[Garlic]], sliced
- 1 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 0.5 tsp [[Turmeric]]
- 7 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 860 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Heat oil in a pot over medium heat. Add cumin and garlic and cook 30 seconds until fragrant but not dark.
2. Add onion, tomato, turmeric, and vegetable and cook 4 minutes until the tomato softens.
3. Add the dal, salt, and 500 ml water. Pressure cook for 3 whistles or simmer covered on low heat for 25 minutes until the dal is soft and spoon-coating.
4. Whisk lightly to break some of the dal while keeping some texture, then serve with the cooked grain.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 15 minutes.
- Cook time: 24 minutes.
- Total time: 39 minutes.
- Calories: 513 per serving.
- Protein: 18 g per serving.
- Carbs: 92 g per serving.
- Fat: 7 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Dal keeps 3 days chilled and freezes well. Reheat over low heat with a splash of water. Cook the grain fresh if possible; if not, cool it quickly so it holds separate grains on reheating.
