# Vazhaithandu Kootu with Brown Rice

## Summary
This midday recipe uses classic Tamil and Karnataka flavor structure while keeping the meal high in fiber and moderate in protein. Vazhaithandu Kootu with Brown Rice brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Brown Rice]], washed
- 80 g [[Moong Dal]], washed
- 240 g [[Banana Stem]], medium dice
- 20 g [[Coconut]], grated
- 1.2 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]], chopped
- 1 tsp [[Mustard Seeds]]
- 10 g [[Urad Dal]]
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 8 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.5 tsp [[Salt]]
- 880 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Cook the vegetable with 250 ml water and salt over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes until just tender.
2. Add the cooked legume and the coconut paste and simmer on low heat for 5 minutes, stirring often so the base does not catch.
3. In a small pan, heat oil on medium heat, crackle mustard and urad dal, then add curry leaves. Pour this tempering over the kootu immediately.
4. Fold once, rest 2 minutes for the flavors to settle, and serve with the cooked grain.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 16 minutes.
- Cook time: 22 minutes.
- Total time: 38 minutes.
- Calories: 557 per serving.
- Protein: 19 g per serving.
- Carbs: 99 g per serving.
- Fat: 10 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Kootu keeps 3 days refrigerated and reheats well over low heat with a splash of water. Freeze only the legume-vegetable base; add the fresh tempering after thawing for a cleaner finish.
