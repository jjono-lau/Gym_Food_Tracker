# Avarakkai Paruppu Usili with Red Rice

## Summary
Traditional tempering and slow simmering deepen the flavor so the meal tastes complete even with minimal oil. Avarakkai Paruppu Usili with Red Rice brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Red Rice]], washed
- 60 g [[Chana Dal]], washed
- 40 g [[Toor Dal]], washed
- 220 g [[Beans]], finely chopped
- 3 tsp [[Dry Red Chilli]]
- 0.2 tsp [[Asafoetida]]
- 1 tsp [[Mustard Seeds]]
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 8 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 620 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Steam or saute the vegetable with a splash of water on medium heat for 5 to 6 minutes until just tender.
2. Heat oil in a kadai, add mustard and curry leaves, then add the crumbled steamed dal mixture and cook on medium-low heat for 4 minutes until dry and grainy.
3. Fold in the cooked vegetable and cook 2 minutes more so the flavors combine.
4. Serve the usili alongside or over the cooked grain as a composed lunch bowl.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 18 minutes.
- Cook time: 24 minutes.
- Total time: 42 minutes.
- Calories: 537 per serving.
- Protein: 19 g per serving.
- Carbs: 99 g per serving.
- Fat: 8 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Usili keeps 3 days refrigerated and reheats well in a dry skillet over low heat. For batch prep, steam and crumble the lentil mixture ahead; finish the final saute just before serving for the best texture.
