# Idiyappam with Vegetable Ishtu

## Summary
Built for an easy night meal, the recipe focuses on soft textures, digestibility, and controlled portions of whole grains. Idiyappam with Vegetable Ishtu brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Red Rice Flour]]
- 200 g [[Bottle Gourd]], thin strips
- 220 ml [[Light Coconut Milk]]
- 60 g [[Green Peas]]
- 60 g [[Onion]], sliced
- 10 g [[Ginger]], julienned
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]], slit
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 6 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 540 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Warm a pot on medium heat with oil. Add onion, ginger, chilli, and curry leaves and soften for 2 minutes without browning.
2. Add the vegetable, peas, salt, and 180 ml water. Cover and cook on medium-low heat for 6 minutes until just tender.
3. Add coconut milk and simmer gently on low heat for 4 minutes until lightly thickened.
4. Serve the hot stew over the idiyappam so the strands absorb flavor but still retain shape.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 14 minutes.
- Cook time: 16 minutes.
- Total time: 30 minutes.
- Calories: 459 per serving.
- Protein: 10 g per serving.
- Carbs: 76 g per serving.
- Fat: 13 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Cooked idiyappam and stew keep 2 days refrigerated when stored separately. Reheat the stew on low heat and steam the idiyappam for 2 minutes. Batch-cook the string hoppers ahead and finish the stew fresh.
