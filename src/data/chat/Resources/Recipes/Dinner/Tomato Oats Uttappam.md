# Tomato Oats Uttappam

## Summary
This dinner keeps the palate warm and savory while leaning on vegetables and lentils more than fat. Tomato Oats Uttappam brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 150 g [[Rolled Oats]]
- 55 g [[Urad Dal]]
- 100 g [[Tomato]], small dice
- 70 g [[Onion]], small dice
- 70 g [[Tomato]], small dice
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 0.5 tsp [[Black Pepper]]
- 8 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 430 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Heat a tawa on medium heat and grease lightly.
2. Pour a ladle of batter into a thick round, scatter the vegetables over the top, and press gently.
3. Cook 3 minutes on medium-low heat until the bottom turns golden and the top starts to set.
4. Flip and cook 1 minute on low heat, then serve with onion chutney.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 12 minutes.
- Cook time: 16 minutes.
- Total time: 28 minutes.
- Calories: 452 per serving.
- Protein: 21 g per serving.
- Carbs: 73 g per serving.
- Fat: 10 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Batter keeps up to 3 days chilled. Reheat cooked uttappam covered on low heat for 2 minutes. Chop toppings fresh for each session so they do not release excess moisture.
