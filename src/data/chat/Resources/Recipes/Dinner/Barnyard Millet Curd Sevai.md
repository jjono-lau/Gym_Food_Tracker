# Barnyard Millet Curd Sevai

## Summary
Built for an easy night meal, the recipe focuses on soft textures, digestibility, and controlled portions of whole grains. Barnyard Millet Curd Sevai brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Barnyard Millet]]
- 100 g [[Cucumber]], fine strips
- 60 g [[Onion]], thin slices
- 18 g [[Coconut]], grated
- 1 tsp [[Mustard Seeds]]
- 10 g [[Urad Dal]]
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]]
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 8 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 260 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Heat a wide pan on medium heat with oil. Add mustard, urad dal, curry leaves, and chilli and cook 45 seconds.
2. Add onion and vegetable and saute 3 minutes until just tender.
3. Add the sevai, salt, and 30 ml water and toss on low heat for 2 minutes.
4. Finish with coconut, toss once, and serve immediately.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 10 minutes.
- Cook time: 12 minutes.
- Total time: 22 minutes.
- Calories: 352 per serving.
- Protein: 11 g per serving.
- Carbs: 53 g per serving.
- Fat: 10 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Sevai keeps 2 days chilled. Reheat covered on low heat with 1 tbsp water. Hold the coconut separately if making ahead so the flavor stays fresh.
