# Kambu Roti with Ridge Gourd Kootu

## Summary
Built for an easy night meal, the recipe focuses on soft textures, digestibility, and controlled portions of whole grains. Kambu Roti with Ridge Gourd Kootu brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 160 g [[Kambu Flour]]
- 220 ml [[Water]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 220 g [[Ridge Gourd]], medium dice
- 70 g [[Moong Dal]]
- 18 g [[Coconut]]
- 1 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]]
- 7 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]

## Steps
1. Cook the vegetable with a splash of water on medium heat until just tender, then add the cooked dal and coconut paste and simmer 5 minutes on low heat.
2. Temper the kootu with oil and curry leaves.
3. Divide the dough into 4 balls, roll each into a thin roti, and cook on a hot tawa over medium-high heat for 1 minute per side until brown spots appear.
4. Serve the rotis hot with the soft kootu.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 18 minutes.
- Cook time: 20 minutes.
- Total time: 38 minutes.
- Calories: 497 per serving.
- Protein: 19 g per serving.
- Carbs: 83 g per serving.
- Fat: 11 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Roti dough keeps 24 hours chilled. Kootu keeps 3 days refrigerated. Reheat rotis wrapped in a cloth over gentle heat and warm the kootu separately with a splash of water.
