# Pearl Millet Idli with Soppu Saaru

## Summary
Built for an easy night meal, the recipe focuses on soft textures, digestibility, and controlled portions of whole grains. Pearl Millet Idli with Soppu Saaru brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 140 g [[Kambu]]
- 55 g [[Urad Dal]]
- 0.5 tsp [[Fenugreek Seeds]]
- 140 g [[Amaranth Leaves]], chopped
- 60 g [[Toor Dal]]
- 90 g [[Tomato]]
- 10 g [[Garlic]]
- 1 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 0.8 tsp [[Black Pepper]]
- 6 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 760 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Steam the idlis on medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes until springy and fully set.
2. For the saaru, simmer dal, tomato, vegetable, garlic, cumin, pepper, salt, and 400 ml water over medium heat for 10 minutes until fragrant and lightly broth-like.
3. Temper with a little oil if desired and pour the hot saaru into bowls.
4. Serve the idlis alongside or submerged briefly so they soak up the broth.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 18 minutes.
- Cook time: 22 minutes.
- Total time: 40 minutes.
- Calories: 508 per serving.
- Protein: 24 g per serving.
- Carbs: 88 g per serving.
- Fat: 8 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Idlis keep 3 days chilled and freeze well. Saaru keeps 2 days. Reheat idlis by steaming and warm the broth separately before combining.
