# Vegetable Kozhukattai in Coconut Broth

## Summary
This dinner keeps the palate warm and savory while leaning on vegetables and lentils more than fat. Vegetable Kozhukattai in Coconut Broth brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 150 g [[Red Rice Flour]]
- 100 g [[Beans]], finely chopped
- 10 g [[Ginger]]
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]]
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]]
- 220 ml [[Light Coconut Milk]]
- 60 g [[Moong Dal]], cooked soft
- 7 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 520 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Steam the shaped dumplings on medium heat for 10 minutes until firm and matte.
2. In a pot, warm oil and saute ginger, chilli, curry leaves, and the vegetable for 2 minutes on medium heat.
3. Add cooked moong dal, water, coconut milk, and salt and simmer on low heat for 5 minutes.
4. Slide in the steamed dumplings and cook 2 minutes more so they absorb some broth before serving.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 16 minutes.
- Cook time: 18 minutes.
- Total time: 34 minutes.
- Calories: 515 per serving.
- Protein: 15 g per serving.
- Carbs: 84 g per serving.
- Fat: 14 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Store dumplings and broth separately for up to 2 days. Reheat the broth first, then add the dumplings for 1 to 2 minutes. This prevents them from swelling and losing shape.
