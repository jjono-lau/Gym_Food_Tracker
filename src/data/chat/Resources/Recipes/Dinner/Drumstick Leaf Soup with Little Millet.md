# Drumstick Leaf Soup with Little Millet

## Summary
This dinner keeps the palate warm and savory while leaning on vegetables and lentils more than fat. Drumstick Leaf Soup with Little Millet brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 260 g [[Drumstick Leaves]], small dice
- 70 g [[Little Millet]]
- 60 g [[Onion]]
- 10 g [[Garlic]]
- 8 g [[Ginger]]
- 0.8 tsp [[Black Pepper]]
- 1 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 6 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 760 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Heat oil in a soup pot over medium heat. Add onion, garlic, ginger, cumin, and pepper and cook 2 minutes until fragrant.
2. Add the vegetable, pulse or grain accent, salt, and water. Bring to a boil, then reduce to low heat and simmer 18 minutes until the vegetables are fully tender.
3. Blend lightly if the soup benefits from body, or leave chunky for a brothier finish.
4. Adjust seasoning and serve hot as a complete light dinner.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 12 minutes.
- Cook time: 22 minutes.
- Total time: 34 minutes.
- Calories: 252 per serving.
- Protein: 16 g per serving.
- Carbs: 39 g per serving.
- Fat: 7 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Soup keeps 3 days chilled and freezes well. Reheat gently to a simmer. If using leafy greens, add them fresh during reheating so the color stays clean.
