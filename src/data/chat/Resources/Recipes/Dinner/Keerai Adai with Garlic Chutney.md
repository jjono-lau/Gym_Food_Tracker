# Keerai Adai with Garlic Chutney

## Summary
The dish uses South Indian evening-tiffin logic: quick-cooking grains, legumes, and aromatic tempering for depth without richness. Keerai Adai with Garlic Chutney brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 70 g [[Toor Dal]]
- 40 g [[Chana Dal]]
- 35 g [[Toor Dal]]
- 20 g [[Urad Dal]]
- 100 g [[Spinach]], finely chopped
- 60 g [[Onion]], finely chopped
- 10 g [[Ginger]]
- 3 tsp [[Dry Red Chilli]]
- 1 tsp [[Cumin Seeds]]
- 9 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 380 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Heat a tawa on medium heat and spread 130 ml batter into a thick round.
2. Drizzle oil around the edge and cook 3 minutes until the underside browns and the top loses its raw sheen.
3. Flip and cook 2 more minutes on medium-low heat until the center is cooked through.
4. Serve hot with garlic chutney.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 12 minutes.
- Cook time: 16 minutes.
- Total time: 28 minutes.
- Calories: 354 per serving.
- Protein: 20 g per serving.
- Carbs: 56 g per serving.
- Fat: 7 g per serving.
- Meal prep: The batter keeps 2 days chilled. Reheat cooked adai in a dry skillet over low heat for 1 minute per side. Make the side dish ahead and cook adai to order for the best texture.
