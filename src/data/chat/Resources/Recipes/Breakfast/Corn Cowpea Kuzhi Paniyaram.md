# Corn Cowpea Kuzhi Paniyaram

## Summary
The recipe uses gentle seasoning and controlled oil so the grain, lentil, and vegetable flavors stay clear and clean. Corn Cowpea Kuzhi Paniyaram brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 220 g [[Cowpea]]
- 100 g [[Corn]], finely chopped
- 70 g [[Onion]], finely chopped
- 10 g [[Ginger]], finely chopped
- 2 tsp [[Green Chilli]], finely chopped
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]], chopped
- 8 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 0.8 tsp [[Salt]]

## Steps
1. Heat the paniyaram pan over medium heat for 2 minutes and brush each cavity lightly with oil.
2. Mix onion, vegetable, ginger, chilli, curry leaves, and salt into the batter. Fill each cavity three-quarters full.
3. Cook covered on medium-low heat for 3 minutes until the edges set and the bottom turns deep golden.
4. Turn each paniyaram with a skewer, cook 2 more minutes until evenly browned and springy, then serve hot.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 10 minutes.
- Cook time: 16 minutes.
- Total time: 26 minutes.
- Calories: 468 per serving.
- Protein: 29 g per serving.
- Carbs: 80 g per serving.
- Fat: 6 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Paniyaram keeps 2 days in the fridge. Reheat in the same mould pan over low heat for 3 minutes or steam briefly. For batch prep, ferment the batter earlier and mix in vegetables only just before cooking.
