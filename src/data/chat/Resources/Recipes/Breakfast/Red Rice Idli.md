# Red Rice Idli

## Summary
This morning dish leans on traditional South Indian technique but shifts the grain balance toward whole grains and lentils. Red Rice Idli brings a distinct structure, seasoning pattern, and grain-legume balance to the table.

## Ingredients
- 130 g [[Red Rice]], washed
- 55 g [[Urad Dal]], washed
- 0.8 tsp [[Fenugreek Seeds]]
- 90 g [[Carrot]], finely grated
- 8 g [[Ginger]], finely grated
- 4 g [[Curry Leaves]], minced
- 4 ml [[Sesame Oil]]
- 1.2 tsp [[Salt]]
- 360 ml [[Water]]

## Steps
1. Grease the idli moulds very lightly with oil and fill each cavity to three-quarters full.
2. Steam over medium heat for 10 to 12 minutes until the tops look dry and a skewer comes out clean.
3. Rest the moulds off heat for 2 minutes so the crumb firms up, then unmould with a wet spoon.
4. Serve hot with tomato chutney; if holding for later, cool on a rack so steam does not make the idlis gummy.

## Nutrition Notes
- Serves 2.
- Prep time: 15 minutes.
- Cook time: 14 minutes.
- Total time: 29 minutes.
- Calories: 369 per serving.
- Protein: 12 g per serving.
- Carbs: 71 g per serving.
- Fat: 4 g per serving.
- Meal prep: Idlis keep 3 days chilled and freeze well for 1 month. Reheat by steaming for 4 minutes or microwaving covered for 30 seconds. Extra idlis can be cubed and turned into idli upma without added oil.
