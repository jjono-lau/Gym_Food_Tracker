# Olympic Flat Bench Press

## Summary
An Olympic Flat Bench Press is a fixed barbell bench station designed for flat pressing with rack hooks and, on some models, spotting supports.

## Key Points
- Do: set your eye line under the bar before you unrack and use a spotter when the load gets challenging.
- Do: keep your shoulder blades set and your feet planted before the bar leaves the hooks.
- Do not: bench heavy alone if the station does not have safeties you trust.
- Do not: rack the bar by searching with straight elbows and a loose upper back.
