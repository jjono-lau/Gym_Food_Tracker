# Incline Bench

## Summary
An Incline Bench raises your torso to shift pressing and curling angles higher than a flat setup.

## Key Points
- Do: choose an angle that lets your shoulders move naturally without shrugging forward.
- Do: keep your upper back anchored to the pad and your feet stable on the floor.
- Do not: set the bench so steep that an incline press turns into a poor overhead press.
- Do not: flare your elbows aggressively if that pushes the shoulder into discomfort.
