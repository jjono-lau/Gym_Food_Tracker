# Hack Squat

## Summary
A Hack Squat guides the body through a squat pattern with shoulder pads and a fixed track.

## Key Points
- Do: set your shoulders fully under the pads and position your feet so your knees can travel naturally.
- Do: lower with control and push through the mid-foot to stand back up.
- Do not: let your heels rise or your knees collapse inward near the bottom.
- Do not: drop into the safeties or bounce off the bottom position.
