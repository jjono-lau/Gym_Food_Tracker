# Decline Bench

## Summary
A Decline Bench lowers your torso angle for decline pressing, sit-up variations, and some core training movements.

## Key Points
- Do: secure your legs fully before starting any loaded or repeated movement on the bench.
- Do: keep the movement controlled because the decline angle increases the risk of sliding.
- Do not: rush set-up or try to get in and out with heavy dumbbells without a clear plan.
- Do not: hyperextend your lower back to force extra range.
