# Glute Press

## Summary
A Glute Press targets hip extension by driving one leg backward or downward against a guided resistance path.

## Key Points
- Do: brace your trunk and keep the working hip square as you press through the heel.
- Do: use a range that lets the glute finish the rep without twisting the pelvis.
- Do not: arch the low back to make the movement look bigger than it is.
- Do not: let the supporting foot and torso drift out of position between reps.
