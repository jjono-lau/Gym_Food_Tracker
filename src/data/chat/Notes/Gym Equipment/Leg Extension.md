# Leg Extension

## Summary
A Leg Extension isolates the quadriceps through knee extension using a seat, back pad, and shin roller.

## Key Points
- Do: line up the machine pivot with your knee joint and place the pad just above the ankle.
- Do: lift with control and pause briefly at the top if your knees tolerate that position well.
- Do not: swing the weight up or slam the stack between repetitions.
- Do not: force painful end range if your knees feel sharp or unstable there.
