# Dumbbells

## Summary
Dumbbells are handheld free weights used for pressing, rowing, carrying, and single-arm or single-leg strength work.

## Key Points
- Do: pick a weight you can control through the full range of motion and keep your wrist stacked over your forearm.
- Do: return each dumbbell to the rack with control and use matching pairs for bilateral lifts.
- Do not: throw dumbbells to the floor unless the space is designed for that use and the exercise specifically allows it.
- Do not: kick heavy dumbbells into position without first setting your back and feet.
