# Recumbent Bike

## Summary
A Recumbent Bike offers supported seated cardio with a backrest and forward pedals.

## Key Points
- Do: slide the seat so your knee remains slightly bent at the furthest pedal position.
- Do: stay back against the seat and pedal at a smooth, repeatable cadence.
- Do not: set the seat so close that your knees jam toward your chest on every stroke.
- Do not: treat the backrest as a cue to slump through the spine and neck.
