# Upright Bike

## Summary
An Upright Bike provides seated cardio with a more vertical cycling posture than a recumbent bike.

## Key Points
- Do: set the saddle so there is a slight knee bend at the bottom of the pedal stroke.
- Do: keep a light grip on the bars and pedal smoothly through the full circle.
- Do not: ride with the seat so low that your knees stay deeply bent the entire time.
- Do not: rock side to side through the hips to create extra force.
