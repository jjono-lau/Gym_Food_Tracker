# Rear Drive Elliptical

## Summary
A Rear Drive Elliptical is a low-impact cardio machine with an elliptical stride path driven from the rear flywheel area.

## Key Points
- Do: keep your heels and hips stable so the stride remains smooth and controlled.
- Do: adjust resistance and incline gradually to keep posture and cadence clean.
- Do not: bounce through the pedals or twist your knees inward as fatigue builds.
- Do not: step off until the pedals come nearly to a stop.
