# EZ Curl Bar

## Summary
An EZ Curl Bar is a curved bar that reduces wrist strain during curls, extensions, and other arm-focused lifts.

## Key Points
- Do: use the grip angle that keeps your wrists neutral and elbows quiet during the movement.
- Do: keep the bar close to your body when curling or extending to maintain control.
- Do not: swing your torso to move the weight once your elbows stop driving the exercise.
- Do not: load the bar unevenly or skip collars when the bar is plate loaded.
