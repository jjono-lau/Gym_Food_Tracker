# Vertical Row

## Summary
A Vertical Row machine trains the upper back through a chest-supported or seated pulling path.

## Key Points
- Do: set the chest or seat support so your shoulders can reach forward without losing position.
- Do: drive the elbows back and down rather than shrugging the shoulders upward.
- Do not: crane your neck forward to meet the handles.
- Do not: shorten the rep so much that the shoulder blades never move through full control.
