# Preacher Curl Bench

## Summary
A Preacher Curl Bench supports the upper arm to isolate elbow flexion during curls and reduce body sway.

## Key Points
- Do: set the seat or standing height so your armpits rest comfortably at the top of the pad.
- Do: lower the weight under control to keep tension on the biceps without yanking the elbow joint.
- Do not: lock your elbows hard at the bottom of each rep.
- Do not: use momentum from your shoulders or lower back to cheat the top half of the curl.
