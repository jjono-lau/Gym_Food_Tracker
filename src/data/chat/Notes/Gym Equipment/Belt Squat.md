# Belt Squat

## Summary
A Belt Squat loads the hips through a waist belt so you can squat hard with less spinal loading than a back squat.

## Key Points
- Do: clip the belt securely, stand tall before taking the load, and use the handles only for balance.
- Do: squat to the depth you can own while keeping the belt centered and your torso stable.
- Do not: hang on the handles to turn the lift into an assisted movement.
- Do not: let the chain or lever start from a twisted position.
