# Stair Climber

## Summary
A Stair Climber is a rotating-step cardio machine that mimics continuous stair ascent.

## Key Points
- Do: place the whole foot on the step, stand tall, and use the rails lightly for balance if needed.
- Do: start slower than you think and build speed only after your rhythm is stable.
- Do not: hang your bodyweight on the front console or side rails.
- Do not: take short, skipping steps that leave the heel off the platform the entire time.
