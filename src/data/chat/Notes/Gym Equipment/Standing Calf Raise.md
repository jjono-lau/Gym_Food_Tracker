# Standing Calf Raise

## Summary
A Standing Calf Raise trains the calf complex from an upright position with shoulder or hip-supported resistance.

## Key Points
- Do: move through a smooth stretch and strong top contraction while keeping the knees softly unlocked.
- Do: use the balls of the feet on the platform and keep the ankles tracking straight.
- Do not: bounce quickly at the bottom of the movement.
- Do not: shift all the pressure to the outer or inner edge of the foot.
