# Weight Collars

## Summary
Weight Collars lock plates in place on barbells and some plate-loaded stations so the load stays balanced during movement.

## Key Points
- Do: clamp collars firmly after every load change, especially for presses, squats, lunges, and carries.
- Do: inspect collars for wear so they still lock securely to the sleeve.
- Do not: rely on friction alone to keep plates in place when the bar is moving quickly or changing angle.
- Do not: use broken or loose collars on heavy lifts.
