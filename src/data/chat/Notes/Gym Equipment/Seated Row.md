# Seated Row

## Summary
A Seated Row machine trains the mid-back and lats by pulling handles toward the torso from a seated position.

## Key Points
- Do: sit tall, set your feet, and start each rep by letting the shoulder blades move under control.
- Do: pull the handle toward the lower ribs or upper abdomen depending on the machine design.
- Do not: round aggressively through the spine to chase extra reach.
- Do not: jerk the first inch of the rep with lower back momentum.
