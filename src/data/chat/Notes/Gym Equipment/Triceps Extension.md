# Triceps Extension

## Summary
A Triceps Extension machine isolates elbow extension using a fixed handle path and supported upper arm position.

## Key Points
- Do: align your elbows with the machine pivot or pad position and keep them stable as you extend.
- Do: squeeze through the finish without snapping violently into lockout.
- Do not: let your elbows drift wide or rise off the pad to cheat the rep.
- Do not: use body English to finish the last few reps.
