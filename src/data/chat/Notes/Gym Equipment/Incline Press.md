# Incline Press

## Summary
An Incline Press machine trains an upward pressing angle that emphasizes the upper chest and front deltoids.

## Key Points
- Do: set the seat so the handles start just below shoulder height and your neck stays relaxed.
- Do: press in the machine path instead of forcing the elbows into a wider arc than the design allows.
- Do not: flare the elbows high if that causes shoulder pinching.
- Do not: drive your head forward off the pad to finish the rep.
