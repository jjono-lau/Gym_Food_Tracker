# Angled Leg Press

## Summary
An Angled Leg Press uses a sled on rails to train the lower body with a fixed diagonal pressing path.

## Key Points
- Do: keep your lower back and hips pressed into the pad while you lower the sled under control.
- Do: place your feet in a stance that lets your knees track cleanly and your heels stay down.
- Do not: unlock the sled until your feet are fully planted and your brace is set.
- Do not: shorten every rep into a partial bounce at the bottom.
