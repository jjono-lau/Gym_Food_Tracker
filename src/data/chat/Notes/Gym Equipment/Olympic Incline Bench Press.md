# Olympic Incline Bench Press

## Summary
An Olympic Incline Bench Press is a fixed station for incline barbell pressing with built-in hooks and a preset bench angle.

## Key Points
- Do: confirm the bench angle and rack height fit your shoulder position before loading heavily.
- Do: unrack with your chest up and bar directly over your shoulder line, not your face.
- Do not: let the bar drift low toward the upper abdomen because the path is steeper than flat pressing.
- Do not: force the station if the angle does not suit your shoulder mobility.
