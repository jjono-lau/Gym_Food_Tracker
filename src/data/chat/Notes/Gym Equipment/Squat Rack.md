# Squat Rack

## Summary
A Squat Rack is a barbell support station for squatting, pressing, rack pulls, and other compound lifts.

## Key Points
- Do: center the bar, set the hooks just below shoulder height, and use safeties when available.
- Do: brace before you unrack and rerack the bar by contacting both uprights evenly.
- Do not: twist into the hooks from one side first when reracking under fatigue.
- Do not: leave plates or benches around the rack where they interfere with footwork.
