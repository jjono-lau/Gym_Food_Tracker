# Pec Fly Rear Deltoid

## Summary
A Pec Fly Rear Deltoid machine trains either chest fly or rear delt fly patterns depending on seat and arm position.

## Key Points
- Do: set the handles and seat for the chosen variation before loading the stack.
- Do: move from the shoulder joint with soft elbows and steady control.
- Do not: over-bend or straighten the elbows repeatedly during the rep.
- Do not: swing your torso to get through the hardest part of the motion.
