# Seated Calf Raise

## Summary
A Seated Calf Raise emphasizes the soleus by training plantar flexion from a bent-knee seated position.

## Key Points
- Do: adjust the thigh pad snugly and use a full ankle range with controlled tempo.
- Do: pause at the top to make the calf do the work instead of rebounding through the rep.
- Do not: rush the bottom stretch or let the knees shift around under the pad.
- Do not: load so heavily that every rep turns into a short partial.
