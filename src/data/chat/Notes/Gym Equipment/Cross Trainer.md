# Cross Trainer

## Summary
A Cross Trainer provides low-impact cardio through a standing stride pattern using moving handles and foot platforms.

## Key Points
- Do: place both feet fully on the pedals before starting and let the machine build rhythm gradually.
- Do: stay tall through the torso and use the handles to share the work between upper and lower body.
- Do not: lean all your weight onto the handles and let the pedals drag your legs.
- Do not: stop pedaling abruptly at high resistance.
