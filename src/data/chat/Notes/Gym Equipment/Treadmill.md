# Treadmill

## Summary
A Treadmill is a moving-belt cardio machine for walking, jogging, running, and incline work.

## Key Points
- Do: clip the safety key, step onto the side rails before the belt starts, and begin at a manageable speed.
- Do: keep your eyes forward and your stride under your center of mass.
- Do not: jump on or off a moving belt.
- Do not: hold the rails for long periods unless you need them briefly to stabilize and reset.
