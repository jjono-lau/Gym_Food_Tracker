# Virtual Bike

## Summary
A Virtual Bike is an indoor cycling bike with adjustable riding position and guided or self-directed ride options.

## Key Points
- Do: set saddle height, saddle fore-aft, and handlebar position before you start the ride.
- Do: keep your knees tracking straight and your effort matched to your conditioning level.
- Do not: sprint out of the saddle before the bike fit feels stable and controlled.
- Do not: mash the pedals at resistance levels that force your hips to rock.
