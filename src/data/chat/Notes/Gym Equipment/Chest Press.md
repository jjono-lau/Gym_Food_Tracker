# Chest Press

## Summary
A Chest Press machine trains horizontal pressing with back support and a guided handle path.

## Key Points
- Do: set the seat so the handles start around mid-chest and your wrists stay neutral.
- Do: keep your shoulder blades lightly set against the pad and press smoothly to near lockout.
- Do not: shrug your shoulders forward as the handles move away from you.
- Do not: slam the handles back into the start position between reps.
