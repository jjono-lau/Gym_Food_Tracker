# Tricep Dip

## Summary
A Tricep Dip machine trains elbow extension and shoulder depression in a supported dipping pattern.

## Key Points
- Do: keep your chest tall and drive the handles down with the triceps instead of collapsing through the shoulders.
- Do: use a range that keeps the shoulder front comfortable and controlled.
- Do not: let the shoulders roll far forward at the bottom.
- Do not: bounce from the stretch position to start the next repetition.
