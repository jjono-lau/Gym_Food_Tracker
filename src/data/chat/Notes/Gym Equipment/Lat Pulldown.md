# Lat Pulldown

## Summary
A Lat Pulldown trains vertical pulling by bringing a bar or handles down toward the upper chest.

## Key Points
- Do: secure the thigh pad, sit tall, and pull the elbows down toward your sides.
- Do: stop near the upper chest or chin level while keeping the torso mostly stable.
- Do not: yank the bar behind your neck.
- Do not: lean so far back that the exercise turns into a row.
