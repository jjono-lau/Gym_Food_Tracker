# Weight Plates

## Summary
Weight Plates are the primary loading pieces for barbells, plate-loaded machines, and storage pegs.

## Key Points
- Do: carry plates close to your body and store them by size so loading stays efficient and safe.
- Do: load plates symmetrically and use the smallest useful jumps to manage progression.
- Do not: leave plates on the floor around racks or benches where they create trip hazards.
- Do not: force plates onto sleeves that are damaged or too tight for safe loading.
