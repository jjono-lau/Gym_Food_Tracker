# Abdominal Crunch

## Summary
An Abdominal Crunch machine trains trunk flexion with external resistance and guided support.

## Key Points
- Do: brace lightly, exhale as you curl the ribs toward the pelvis, and keep the movement focused through the trunk.
- Do: start with a load that lets you feel the abs working without neck strain.
- Do not: yank on pads or handles with your arms to fake extra abdominal work.
- Do not: turn the rep into a hip hinge instead of a controlled crunch.
