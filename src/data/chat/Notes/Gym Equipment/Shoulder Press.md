# Shoulder Press

## Summary
A Shoulder Press machine trains vertical pressing with guided resistance and seated trunk support.

## Key Points
- Do: set the seat so the handles begin near ear level and the wrists stack under the forearms.
- Do: keep ribs down and the back supported while pressing overhead.
- Do not: turn the rep into a back bend to chase more load.
- Do not: start from a position that forces your shoulders behind your comfortable range.
