# Flat Bench

## Summary
A Flat Bench provides stable support for dumbbell work, barbell work, and many seated or prone upper body lifts.

## Key Points
- Do: plant your feet before starting the set and keep the bench centered under the lift.
- Do: use it for controlled pressing, rowing, fly variations, and supported arm work.
- Do not: bridge excessively or slide your shoulders off the pad to shorten the movement.
- Do not: leave dumbbells standing upright on the bench between sets.
