# Leg Press

## Summary
A Leg Press trains the quads, glutes, and calves with back support and a guided lower body pressing path.

## Key Points
- Do: set the seat so your knees can bend deeply without your lower back rolling off the pad.
- Do: drive through the full foot and keep your knees tracking over your toes.
- Do not: lock your knees hard at the top of the rep.
- Do not: lower the sled so far that your hips tuck under and your low back rounds.
