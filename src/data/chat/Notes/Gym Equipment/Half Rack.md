# Half Rack

## Summary
A Half Rack is an open rack for barbell squats, presses, pulls, and accessory work with hooks and safeties.

## Key Points
- Do: set the J-hooks and safeties to the correct height before loading the bar.
- Do: walk the bar out with a stable brace and only as many steps as you need.
- Do not: leave the safeties too low to help if you miss the rep.
- Do not: crowd the uprights so closely that you clip them on the unrack or rerack.
