# Barbells

## Summary
Barbells are long straight bars loaded with plates for compound lifts such as squats, presses, rows, and deadlifts.

## Key Points
- Do: load both sides evenly, secure the plates with collars, and check the bar path before your work sets.
- Do: use a rack, safeties, or spotter when the lift can trap you under the bar.
- Do not: assume every bar weighs the same; confirm the bar weight if you are tracking load precisely.
- Do not: twist the bar out of the rack unevenly or walk it out with loose feet and a soft brace.
