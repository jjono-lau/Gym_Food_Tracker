# Adjustable Back Extension

## Summary
An Adjustable Back Extension supports hip hinge and trunk extension work for the lower back, glutes, and hamstrings.

## Key Points
- Do: adjust the pad so it sits below the hips and allows your spine to move from a neutral base.
- Do: move by hinging at the hips and finishing with a straight line from head to heel.
- Do not: swing through the top or crank into lumbar hyperextension.
- Do not: hold a weight plate before you can control bodyweight repetitions cleanly.
