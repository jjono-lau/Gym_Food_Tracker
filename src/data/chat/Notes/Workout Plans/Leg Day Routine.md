# Leg Day Routine

## Summary
Leg Day Routine is a basic lower-body session that trains quads, hamstrings, glutes, and calves with simple machine choices and steady progression.

## Key Points
- Warm up with 5 to 10 minutes on [[Treadmill]], [[Cross Trainer]], or [[Stair Climber]].
- Start with [[Leg Press]] or [[Angled Leg Press]] for 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Leg Extension]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Leg Curl]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Use [[Glute Press]], [[Hack Squat]], or [[Belt Squat]] for 2 to 3 sets of 8 to 10 reps depending on confidence and recovery.
- Finish with [[Standing Calf Raise]] or [[Seated Calf Raise]] for 2 to 3 sets of 12 to 15 reps.
