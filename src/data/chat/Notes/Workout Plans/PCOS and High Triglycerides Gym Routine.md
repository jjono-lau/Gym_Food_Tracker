# PCOS and High Triglycerides Gym Routine

## Summary
PCOS and High Triglycerides Gym Routine is a balanced weekly plan that combines moderate cardio, machine-based strength work, and repeatable training habits for metabolic health.

## Key Points
- Use 4 weekly sessions with 2 cardio-focused days and 2 mixed strength-cardio days.
- Cardio day: 25 to 35 minutes on [[Treadmill]], [[Cross Trainer]], or [[Recumbent Bike]] at a steady moderate pace.
- Mixed day: [[Leg Press]], [[Chest Press]], [[Seated Row]], and [[Abdominal Crunch]] for 2 to 3 sets each, then 10 to 15 minutes on [[Upright Bike]] or [[Treadmill]].
- Keep workouts controlled, leave some reps in reserve, and progress by small increases in time or load.
- Favor routines you can repeat for months rather than short bursts of very hard training.
