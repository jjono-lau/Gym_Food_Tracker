# PCOS Gym Routine

## Summary
PCOS Gym Routine is a basic weekly structure that combines moderate cardio, full-body resistance work, and repeatable recovery-friendly pacing.

## Key Points
- Use 3 to 4 weekly sessions built around consistency rather than maximal effort.
- Session A: 20 minutes on [[Treadmill]] or [[Cross Trainer]], then [[Leg Press]], [[Chest Press]], and [[Seated Row]] for 2 to 3 sets each.
- Session B: 20 to 30 minutes on [[Upright Bike]] or [[Recumbent Bike]], then [[Shoulder Press]], [[Leg Curl]], and [[Abdominal Crunch]] for 2 to 3 sets each.
- Keep most sets in a moderate rep range such as 8 to 12 reps, and stop before form slips.
- Add short walks on non-gym days if possible because total movement across the week matters.
