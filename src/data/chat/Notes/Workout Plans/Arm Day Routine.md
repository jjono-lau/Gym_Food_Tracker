# Arm Day Routine

## Summary
Arm Day Routine is a basic gym session built around direct biceps and triceps work with moderate volume and simple machine or free-weight choices.

## Key Points
- Warm up with 5 to 10 minutes on [[Treadmill]] or [[Cross Trainer]] before the first working set.
- Start with [[Biceps Curl]] or [[Preacher Curl Bench]] for 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Bilateral Arm Curl]] or [[EZ Curl Bar]] curls for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Use [[Triceps Extension]] for 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Tricep Dip]] for 2 to 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps with controlled tempo.
- Finish with 5 to 10 minutes of easy walking or cycling to cool down.
