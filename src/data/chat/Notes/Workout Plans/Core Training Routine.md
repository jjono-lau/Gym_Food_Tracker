# Core Training Routine

## Summary
Core Training Routine is a simple session for trunk strength and control using machine-based core work plus brief cardio warm-up.

## Key Points
- Warm up with 5 minutes on [[Treadmill]] or [[Recumbent Bike]] before core work.
- Use [[Abdominal Crunch]] for 3 sets of 10 to 15 controlled reps.
- Add [[Rotary Torso]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps per side with moderate range of motion.
- Use [[Back Extension]] or [[Adjustable Back Extension]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Keep the movement controlled and avoid loading trunk rotation or extension too aggressively.
- Finish with easy walking and relaxed breathing rather than jumping straight into a maximal lift.
