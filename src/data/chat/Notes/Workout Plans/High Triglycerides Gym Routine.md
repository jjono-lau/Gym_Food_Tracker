# High Triglycerides Gym Routine

## Summary
High Triglycerides Gym Routine is a basic weekly plan that prioritizes regular aerobic work, steady strength training, and sustainable volume.

## Key Points
- Aim for 4 to 5 weekly sessions with cardio appearing most days in some form.
- Session A: 25 to 35 minutes on [[Treadmill]], [[Cross Trainer]], or [[Recumbent Bike]] at moderate intensity.
- Session B: [[Leg Press]], [[Chest Press]], [[Seated Row]], and [[Shoulder Press]] for 2 to 3 sets each, followed by 10 minutes of easy cardio.
- Session C: 20 to 30 minutes on [[Stair Climber]], [[Upright Bike]], or [[Virtual Bike]] with smooth pacing rather than all-out effort.
- Increase total weekly minutes gradually and keep long gaps without activity as low as possible.
