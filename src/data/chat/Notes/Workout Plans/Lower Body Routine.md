# Lower Body Routine

## Summary
Lower Body Routine is a balanced lower-body workout that builds strength and work capacity without requiring an advanced barbell setup.

## Key Points
- Warm up with 5 to 10 minutes on [[Treadmill]], [[Upright Bike]], or [[Cross Trainer]].
- Start with [[Leg Press]], [[Hack Squat]], or [[Belt Squat]] for 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Leg Curl]] for 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Leg Extension]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 12 reps.
- Use [[Glute Press]] or [[Abduction Adduction Machine]] for 2 to 3 sets of 10 to 15 reps.
- Finish with calf work and a short cooldown walk.
