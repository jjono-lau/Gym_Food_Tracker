# Cardio Routine

## Summary
Cardio Routine is a basic gym plan for building weekly aerobic volume with machines that are easy to scale up or down.

## Key Points
- Start with 20 to 30 minutes on [[Treadmill]], [[Cross Trainer]], [[Upright Bike]], or [[Recumbent Bike]] at a pace you can sustain.
- Use 1 to 2 harder intervals only after a steady base feels comfortable, such as short pushes on [[Stair Climber]] or [[Virtual Bike]].
- A simple weekly pattern is 3 to 5 cardio sessions with most work done at moderate intensity.
- Increase time before intensity when you need a more sustainable progression path.
- Finish with 3 to 5 slower minutes to bring effort down gradually.
