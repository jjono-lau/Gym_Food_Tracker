# Basic Gym Plan

## Summary
Basic Gym Plan is a simple 3-day weekly gym structure that uses cardio and machine-based strength work to build consistency, full-body coverage, and safer progression.

## Key Points
- Day 1: Start with 10 minutes on [[Treadmill]] or [[Cross Trainer]], then use [[Leg Press]], [[Chest Press]], [[Seated Row]], and [[Abdominal Crunch]] for 2 to 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps each.
- Day 2: Start with 20 to 30 minutes on [[Upright Bike]], [[Recumbent Bike]], or [[Stair Climber]] at a steady pace, then finish with light mobility or an easy cooldown walk.
- Day 3: Start with 10 minutes on [[Treadmill]] or [[Cross Trainer]], then use [[Leg Curl]], [[Leg Extension]], [[Shoulder Press]], and [[Lat Pulldown]] for 2 to 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps each.
- Rest at least 1 minute between moderate sets and use a load that leaves 1 to 2 good reps in reserve.
- The machine choices keep setup simple, train major muscle groups, and make it easier to repeat the routine consistently.
- The cardio day improves work capacity and recovery while also supporting heart-health and metabolic goals.
