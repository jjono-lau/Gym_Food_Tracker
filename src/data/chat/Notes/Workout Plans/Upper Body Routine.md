# Upper Body Routine

## Summary
Upper Body Routine is a balanced session for chest, shoulders, back, and arms using a small number of machines and free-weight options.

## Key Points
- Warm up with 5 to 10 minutes on [[Cross Trainer]] or [[Treadmill]] and 1 light setup set for each first exercise.
- Use [[Chest Press]] or [[Incline Press]] for 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps.
- Add [[Seated Row]] or [[Lat Pulldown]] for 3 sets of 8 to 12 reps.
- Use [[Shoulder Press]] for 2 to 3 sets of 8 to 10 reps.
- Add one arm movement such as [[Biceps Curl]] and one triceps movement such as [[Triceps Extension]] for 2 sets each.
- Keep rest periods moderate and stop 1 to 2 reps before form breaks down.
