# PCOS Exercise Guidance

## Summary
PCOS Exercise Guidance emphasizes consistent aerobic activity and strength training because exercise can improve symptoms, mood, weight-related outcomes, and metabolic health.

## Key Points
- A practical target is at least 150 minutes of moderate-intensity exercise each week or 75 minutes of vigorous exercise, plus strength training on 2 days each week.
- Walking, treadmill work, cycling, cross training, and stair climbing can all fit the aerobic side of the plan.
- Strength work with free weights and machines is useful because building muscle supports insulin sensitivity, physical function, and long-term metabolic health.
- Daily movement outside formal workouts also matters, such as short walks, stairs, and reduced sedentary time.
- Consistency matters more than perfection, so the best plan is one you can repeat safely and recover from.
