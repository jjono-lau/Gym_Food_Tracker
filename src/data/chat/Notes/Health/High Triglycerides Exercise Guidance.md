# High Triglycerides Exercise Guidance

## Summary
High Triglycerides Exercise Guidance focuses on regular aerobic activity, resistance training, and lower sedentary time because physical activity helps improve triglycerides and heart health.

## Key Points
- Regular physical activity can help lower triglycerides, improve heart and lung fitness, and support weight management.
- A practical baseline is consistent weekly cardio plus resistance training, built up gradually if current fitness is low.
- Useful gym options include treadmill walking, bikes, cross trainers, stair climbers, and progressive free-weight or machine-based strength training.
- Reducing long periods of sitting also matters, so shorter movement breaks during the day are helpful.
- If triglycerides are very high or other medical problems are present, exercise intensity should be discussed with a clinician before starting a harder program.
