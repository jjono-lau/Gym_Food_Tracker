# Treadmill for PCOS Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Treadmill]] for PCOS training with a focus on consistency, insulin sensitivity, and sustainable workload.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Treadmill]] sits inside [[Cardio Equipment Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[PCOS Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Favor repeatable moderate cardio or manageable strength work over all-out sessions you cannot recover from.
- Priority: Use this equipment as part of a weekly plan that combines aerobic work, resistance training, and lower sedentary time.
- Priority: Progress by small increases in time, load, or total work so training supports long-term adherence.
- Best use: treat [[Treadmill]] as a steady cardio option for building weekly training minutes.
- Good pattern: use intervals only after you can already sustain moderate, repeatable sessions comfortably.
- Avoid: turning every session into maximal effort, because missed sessions reduce the long-term benefit.
