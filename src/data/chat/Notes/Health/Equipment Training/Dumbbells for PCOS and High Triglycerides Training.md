# Dumbbells for PCOS and High Triglycerides Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Dumbbells]] for combined PCOS and high triglycerides training with a focus on aerobic base work, strength training, and repeatable weekly structure.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Dumbbells]] sits inside [[Free Weights Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[PCOS Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Use this equipment to support both metabolic health and gym adherence rather than chasing extreme intensity.
- Priority: A balanced mix of cardio, resistance training, and day-to-day movement is usually more useful than specializing too early.
- Priority: Build a plan you can repeat for months, because combined metabolic goals respond best to steady habits.
- Best use: use [[Dumbbells]] for compound lifts and simple accessory work that can be progressed gradually.
- Good pattern: prioritize full-body or upper-lower sessions with controlled sets and repeatable loads.
- Avoid: jumping load too fast or training to technical failure on every set.
