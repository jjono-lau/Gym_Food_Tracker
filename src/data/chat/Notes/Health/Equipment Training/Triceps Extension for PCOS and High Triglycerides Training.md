## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Triceps Extension]] for combined PCOS and high triglycerides training with a focus on aerobic base work, strength training, and repeatable weekly structure.

- Priority: Build a plan you can repeat for months, because combined metabolic goals respond best to steady habits.
- Best use: use [[Triceps Extension]] for controlled resistance training that improves muscle mass, work capacity, and exercise adherence.
