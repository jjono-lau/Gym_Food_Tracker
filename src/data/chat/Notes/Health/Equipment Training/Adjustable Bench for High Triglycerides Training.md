# Adjustable Bench for High Triglycerides Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Adjustable Bench]] for high triglycerides training with a focus on regular aerobic work, steady progression, and heart-health support.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Adjustable Bench]] sits inside [[Benches and Racks Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[High Triglycerides Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Use this equipment to build regular weekly activity because consistency matters more than occasional very hard sessions.
- Priority: Cardio volume and steady resistance training both support triglyceride management when done repeatedly over time.
- Priority: Keep sessions controlled and sustainable so training becomes part of a long-term routine.
- Best use: use [[Adjustable Bench]] to support pressing, rowing, setup quality, and safe progressive overload.
- Good pattern: pair it with cardio or machine work inside a broader weekly plan rather than treating it as the whole program.
- Avoid: using setup equipment as a substitute for the main movement quality you are trying to build.
