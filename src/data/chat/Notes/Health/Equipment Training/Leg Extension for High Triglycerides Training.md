# Leg Extension for High Triglycerides Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Leg Extension]] for high triglycerides training with a focus on regular aerobic work, steady progression, and heart-health support.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Leg Extension]] sits inside [[Lower Body Machines Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[High Triglycerides Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Use this equipment to build regular weekly activity because consistency matters more than occasional very hard sessions.
- Priority: Cardio volume and steady resistance training both support triglyceride management when done repeatedly over time.
- Priority: Keep sessions controlled and sustainable so training becomes part of a long-term routine.
- Best use: use [[Leg Extension]] for controlled resistance training that improves muscle mass, work capacity, and exercise adherence.
- Good pattern: keep the movement controlled, use moderate working sets, and progress the load gradually.
- Avoid: chasing sloppy reps or heavy ego lifting that disrupts recovery and consistency.
