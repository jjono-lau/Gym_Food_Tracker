# Flat Bench for PCOS Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Flat Bench]] for PCOS training with a focus on consistency, insulin sensitivity, and sustainable workload.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Flat Bench]] sits inside [[Benches and Racks Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[PCOS Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Favor repeatable moderate cardio or manageable strength work over all-out sessions you cannot recover from.
- Priority: Use this equipment as part of a weekly plan that combines aerobic work, resistance training, and lower sedentary time.
- Priority: Progress by small increases in time, load, or total work so training supports long-term adherence.
- Best use: use [[Flat Bench]] to support pressing, rowing, setup quality, and safe progressive overload.
- Good pattern: pair it with cardio or machine work inside a broader weekly plan rather than treating it as the whole program.
- Avoid: using setup equipment as a substitute for the main movement quality you are trying to build.
