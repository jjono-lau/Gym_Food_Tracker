# Weight Collars for High Triglycerides Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Weight Collars]] for high triglycerides training with a focus on regular aerobic work, steady progression, and heart-health support.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Weight Collars]] sits inside [[Free Weights Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[High Triglycerides Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Use this equipment to build regular weekly activity because consistency matters more than occasional very hard sessions.
- Priority: Cardio volume and steady resistance training both support triglyceride management when done repeatedly over time.
- Priority: Keep sessions controlled and sustainable so training becomes part of a long-term routine.
- Best use: use [[Weight Collars]] for compound lifts and simple accessory work that can be progressed gradually.
- Good pattern: prioritize full-body or upper-lower sessions with controlled sets and repeatable loads.
- Avoid: jumping load too fast or training to technical failure on every set.
