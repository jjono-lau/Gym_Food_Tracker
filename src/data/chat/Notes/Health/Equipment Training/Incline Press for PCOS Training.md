# Incline Press for PCOS Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Incline Press]] for PCOS training with a focus on consistency, insulin sensitivity, and sustainable workload.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Incline Press]] sits inside [[Upper Body Machines Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[PCOS Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Favor repeatable moderate cardio or manageable strength work over all-out sessions you cannot recover from.
- Priority: Use this equipment as part of a weekly plan that combines aerobic work, resistance training, and lower sedentary time.
- Priority: Progress by small increases in time, load, or total work so training supports long-term adherence.
- Best use: use [[Incline Press]] for controlled resistance training that improves muscle mass, work capacity, and exercise adherence.
- Good pattern: keep the movement controlled, use moderate working sets, and progress the load gradually.
- Avoid: chasing sloppy reps or heavy ego lifting that disrupts recovery and consistency.
