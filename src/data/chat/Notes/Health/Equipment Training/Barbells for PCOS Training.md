# Barbells for PCOS Training

## Summary
This note explains how to use [[Barbells]] for PCOS training with a focus on consistency, insulin sensitivity, and sustainable workload.

## Key Points
- Main context: [[Barbells]] sits inside [[Free Weights Hub]] and should be used within the broader guidance in [[PCOS Exercise Guidance]].
- Priority: Favor repeatable moderate cardio or manageable strength work over all-out sessions you cannot recover from.
- Priority: Use this equipment as part of a weekly plan that combines aerobic work, resistance training, and lower sedentary time.
- Priority: Progress by small increases in time, load, or total work so training supports long-term adherence.
- Best use: use [[Barbells]] for compound lifts and simple accessory work that can be progressed gradually.
- Good pattern: prioritize full-body or upper-lower sessions with controlled sets and repeatable loads.
- Avoid: jumping load too fast or training to technical failure on every set.
