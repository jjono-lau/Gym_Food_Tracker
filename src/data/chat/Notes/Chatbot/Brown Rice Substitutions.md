# Brown Rice Substitutions

## Summary
Brown Rice Substitutions explains which grains can replace brown rice when a recipe needs a whole-grain base with similar meal-planning value.

## Key Points
- Good substitutes for [[Brown Rice]] include [[Red Rice]], [[Foxtail Millet]], [[Little Millet]], [[Barnyard Millet]], and [[Quinoa]].
- [[Red Rice]] is usually the closest whole-grain style swap when the goal is a chewy rice base.
- Millets work well when the dish is a grain bowl, pongal-style dish, or rice-side format, but texture will usually be lighter and less chewy.
- [[Quinoa]] works when the user wants a faster-cooking grain, but the flavor and mouthfeel are different from rice.
- The chatbot should explain that cooking time and water ratio may change even when the meal still works.
