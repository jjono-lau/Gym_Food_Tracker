# Coconut Substitutions

## Summary
Coconut Substitutions explains how to replace coconut when the exact form is missing and when the dish can tolerate a lighter or less rich result.

## Key Points
- [[Light Coconut Milk]] can sometimes replace [[Coconut]] in chutney, stew, or curry-style dishes if the chatbot explains the texture change.
- In some recipes, using less coconut and relying more on the other base ingredients is better than forcing a poor replacement.
- Coconut swaps usually change richness, sweetness, and mouthfeel even when the dish still works.
- If the user asks for a triglyceride-aware direction, the chatbot should frame coconut use more carefully rather than treating it as a neutral ingredient.
- The chatbot should say when coconut is part of the identity of the dish rather than just a background ingredient.
