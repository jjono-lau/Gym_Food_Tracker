# What Is PCOS FAQ

## Summary
What Is PCOS FAQ is a short plain-language entry for chatbot answers about what PCOS is and why it affects food and exercise recommendations.

## Key Points
- PCOS is a hormonal and metabolic condition that can affect periods, ovulation, androgen levels, and insulin sensitivity.
- It can show up through irregular cycles, acne, excess hair growth, fertility issues, or trouble with weight and blood sugar regulation.
- Food and exercise matter because they can help improve insulin sensitivity, cardiometabolic health, and symptom management.
- The chatbot should keep answers simple first, then offer links to food and exercise guidance if the user asks what to do next.
- Diagnosis and treatment still belong with a clinician, not the chatbot.
