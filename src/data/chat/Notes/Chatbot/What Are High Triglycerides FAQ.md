# What Are High Triglycerides FAQ

## Summary
What Are High Triglycerides FAQ is a short plain-language entry for chatbot answers about what triglycerides are and why lifestyle guidance matters.

## Key Points
- Triglycerides are a type of fat in the blood, and higher levels can raise cardiovascular risk.
- Very high triglycerides can also raise the risk of pancreatitis, which is why severe cases need medical attention.
- Food quality, sugar intake, alcohol intake, physical activity, and body weight can all influence triglyceride levels.
- The chatbot should explain the basics clearly and then connect users to food and exercise guidance when they ask what to do.
- Lab interpretation, diagnosis, and medication questions should be escalated beyond chatbot-only answers.
