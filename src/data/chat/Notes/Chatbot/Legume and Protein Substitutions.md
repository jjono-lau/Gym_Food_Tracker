# Legume and Protein Substitutions

## Summary
Legume and Protein Substitutions lists practical swaps for lentils, dals, legumes, and related protein-supporting ingredients used in the meal vault.

## Key Points
- [[Moong Dal]], [[Split Moong Dal]], [[Toor Dal]], and [[Chana Dal]] can sometimes substitute for one another in soups, kootu-style dishes, or dal-style meals, but they change texture and cooking time.
- [[Green Gram]], [[Black Chana]], [[Black Eyed Peas]], and [[Cowpea]] can often substitute for one another in curry or sundal-style dishes when the chatbot notes the different firmness.
- [[Urad Dal]] is often structurally important in batters, so the chatbot should be cautious about replacing it directly in dosa or idli-style recipes.
- [[Low Fat Curd]] and [[Low Fat Buttermilk]] can sometimes swap depending on whether the recipe needs thickness or a lighter liquid texture.
- The chatbot should prefer legume swaps that keep the dish protein-supportive and familiar rather than forcing a totally different result.
