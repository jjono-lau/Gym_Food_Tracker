# High Triglycerides Ingredient Swap Pack

## Summary
High Triglycerides Ingredient Swap Pack defines ingredient substitutions the chatbot should favor when the goal is reducing excess sugar, improving food quality, and supporting better lipid control.

## Key Points
- Prefer high-fiber grains, legumes, vegetables, and minimally processed ingredients over sugary or heavily refined foods.
- Cut back sugary drinks, desserts, and refined snack foods first because these can raise triglycerides quickly.
- Prefer leaner cooking patterns and avoid unnecessary deep-fried substitutions when a baked, steamed, sauteed, or boiled option works.
- Keep alcohol caution in mind when answering triglyceride-related food questions because alcohol can worsen triglyceride levels.
- The chatbot should focus on repeatable eating patterns rather than framing one ingredient as a cure.
