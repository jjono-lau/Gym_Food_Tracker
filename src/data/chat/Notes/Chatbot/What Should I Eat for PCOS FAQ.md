# What Should I Eat for PCOS FAQ

## Summary
What Should I Eat for PCOS FAQ is a direct-answer note for chatbot questions about food choices that support PCOS management.

## Key Points
- A useful answer should emphasize minimally processed foods, higher-fiber carbohydrates, protein support, and balanced meals.
- The chatbot should avoid presenting one perfect PCOS diet and instead suggest practical meal patterns that can be sustained.
- Sugary foods and heavily refined carbohydrates are common targets for reduction when the user wants steadier energy and better insulin support.
- The best follow-up is usually a small number of meal suggestions or ingredient swaps, not a very long food list.
- The chatbot should connect answers to existing recipe and ingredient notes whenever possible.
