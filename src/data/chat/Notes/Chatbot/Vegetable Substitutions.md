# Vegetable Substitutions

## Summary
Vegetable Substitutions lists practical swaps for common vegetables when the exact produce item is missing but the dish can still work.

## Key Points
- [[Cabbage]], [[Cauliflower]], [[Beans]], and [[Capsicum]] often swap well in mixed vegetable dishes where texture variety matters more than one exact vegetable.
- [[Bottle Gourd]], [[Ash Gourd]], and [[Chayote]] can often replace one another in softer cooked dishes, stews, or kootu-style meals because they cook into a similar mild profile.
- [[Beetroot]], [[Carrot]], and [[Pumpkin]] may substitute for color and body in some cooked dishes, but the chatbot should note differences in sweetness and softness.
- [[Spinach]], [[Amaranth Leaves]], and [[Drumstick Leaves]] can sometimes substitute for one another in cooked leafy dishes, though bitterness and texture can change.
- The chatbot should say when a vegetable is central to the identity of a dish, because a workable swap may still make it a different meal.
