# Urad Dal Substitutions

## Summary
Urad Dal Substitutions explains when urad dal can be replaced and when it is too important to the structure of the dish to swap casually.

## Key Points
- [[Urad Dal]] is often structurally important in dosa, idli, and similar fermented batters, so direct substitution is limited.
- In batter-based recipes, the chatbot should usually warn that swapping urad dal changes fermentation, softness, and lift.
- For some non-batter recipes, small amounts of other dals may support the dish, but they should not be presented as equivalent.
- If the user is missing urad dal for a batter-heavy recipe, the better answer may be to choose a different recipe instead of forcing a weak swap.
- The chatbot should be more cautious with urad dal than with many other legumes.
