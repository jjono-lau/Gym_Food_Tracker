# Health Education

## Summary
Health Education defines how the chatbot should answer plain-language health questions using the vault's condition and guidance notes.

## Key Points
- The chatbot should answer educational questions about what PCOS is, what high triglycerides are, and what food or exercise habits are commonly recommended.
- The main vault sources for these answers are [[Health Index Hub]], [[PCOS]], and [[High Triglycerides]].
- Good health education answers should be simple, accurate, and practical, then link back to food or workout guidance when the user asks what to do next.
- The chatbot should say when a question needs a clinician rather than a vault-based answer, especially for diagnosis, medication, severe symptoms, or emergency risk.
- The chatbot should avoid promising outcomes or giving individualized medical treatment plans.
