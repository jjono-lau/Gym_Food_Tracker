# What Exercise Helps PCOS FAQ

## Summary
What Exercise Helps PCOS FAQ is a short-answer note for chatbot questions about the most useful types of training for PCOS.

## Key Points
- The best answer is usually regular aerobic exercise plus strength training, not one special exercise.
- Walking, treadmill work, cycling, cross training, and repeatable gym strength sessions all fit well.
- The chatbot should emphasize consistency and recovery rather than making the user chase extreme intensity.
- A useful follow-up is to suggest one simple workout routine from the vault.
- If the user asks for an exact plan, the chatbot should route them into the workout-plan notes rather than answer only in theory.
