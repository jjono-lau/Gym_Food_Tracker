# Meal Suggestions

## Summary
Meal Suggestions defines how the chatbot should answer recipe and meal-planning questions using the vault's recipe, ingredient, and health notes.

## Key Points
- The chatbot should answer questions about what to eat for breakfast, lunch, dinner, snacks, and health-focused meal ideas.
- The main vault sources for meal answers are [[Meal Planning Hub]], [[Shopping List]], and [[Health Index Hub]].
- Good meal answers should mention relevant recipes, explain why they fit the goal, and suggest a small number of realistic options rather than listing everything.
- For PCOS or high triglycerides, the chatbot should connect recipe suggestions back to [[PCOS Food Guidance]] or [[High Triglycerides Food Guidance]].
- The chatbot should avoid giving extreme diet rules or pretending one meal fixes a health condition by itself.
