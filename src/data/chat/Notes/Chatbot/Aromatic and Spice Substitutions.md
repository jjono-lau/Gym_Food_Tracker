# Aromatic and Spice Substitutions

## Summary
Aromatic and Spice Substitutions lists practical swaps for onion, garlic, ginger, chilli, curry leaves, and ground or whole spice ingredients.

## Key Points
- [[Onion]], [[Ginger]], and [[Garlic]] often support the flavor base, so partial omission is usually easier than a fake direct replacement.
- [[Green Chilli]], [[Dry Red Chilli]], and [[Chilli Powder]] can often substitute for heat level, but the chatbot should note that the flavor profile changes.
- [[Black Pepper]] can replace some chilli heat in pepper-forward dishes, especially when the goal is warmth rather than sharp chilli flavor.
- [[Curry Leaves]], [[Mustard Seeds]], and [[Cumin Seeds]] are often identity-building South Indian flavor notes, so swaps should be framed as approximation rather than exact match.
- [[Asafoetida]] is a small-quantity flavor support ingredient and is usually better omitted than replaced with a random substitute when unavailable.
