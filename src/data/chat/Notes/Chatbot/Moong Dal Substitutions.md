# Moong Dal Substitutions

## Summary
Moong Dal Substitutions explains which legumes can replace moong dal when a dish needs a soft-cooking, mild lentil component.

## Key Points
- Good substitutes for [[Moong Dal]] can include [[Split Moong Dal]], [[Toor Dal]], or [[Chana Dal]] depending on the dish.
- [[Split Moong Dal]] is usually the closest swap because the flavor and cooking behavior are similar.
- [[Toor Dal]] works in soups, dal-style dishes, and some kootu preparations, though the texture is slightly different.
- [[Chana Dal]] can work in some savory dishes but stays firmer and changes the final feel of the meal.
- The chatbot should mention that moong dal is milder and softer than many other dals, so swaps are not fully identical.
