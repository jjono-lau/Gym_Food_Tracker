# Green Chilli Substitutions

## Summary
Green Chilli Substitutions explains how to replace green chilli when the goal is keeping some heat in the dish without the exact same flavor.

## Key Points
- [[Dry Red Chilli]] or [[Chilli Powder]] can often replace part of the heat provided by [[Green Chilli]].
- The chatbot should explain that green chilli gives a fresher flavor while dry chilli and chilli powder give a different kind of heat.
- [[Black Pepper]] can sometimes support warmth when the user wants less sharp chilli flavor.
- If heat is not central to the recipe, reducing spice may be better than forcing a poor substitute.
- The chatbot should avoid promising a one-to-one flavor match.
