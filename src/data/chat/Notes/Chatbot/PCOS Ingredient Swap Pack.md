# PCOS Ingredient Swap Pack

## Summary
PCOS Ingredient Swap Pack defines ingredient substitutions the chatbot should favor when helping with meals for insulin sensitivity and steadier energy.

## Key Points
- Prefer higher-fiber grains and grain alternatives such as millets, oats, brown rice, red rice, and quinoa over heavily refined starches when possible.
- Prefer legumes, lentils, and protein-supporting ingredients when a meal looks too carb-heavy and low in satiety.
- Reduce sugary drinks, sweetened snacks, and dessert-style add-ons when the user asks for a more PCOS-friendly direction.
- Prefer ingredient swaps that make a meal more balanced rather than simply smaller.
- The chatbot should keep swaps realistic for the recipe instead of forcing a health swap that makes the dish stop working.
