# Onion Substitutions

## Summary
Onion Substitutions explains when onion can be reduced, omitted, or loosely replaced in recipes where it supports the flavor base.

## Key Points
- [[Onion]] is often part of the flavor foundation, so omission is sometimes more honest than pretending there is a direct equal substitute.
- In mixed vegetable dishes, increasing another aromatic or vegetable may help the dish still feel complete, but the flavor will change.
- The chatbot should use onion swaps cautiously in simple dishes where onion sweetness and body matter a lot.
- If the user cannot use onion, the better answer may be to suggest recipes that do not rely on it heavily.
- The chatbot should explain that onion contributes sweetness, moisture, and background flavor rather than just volume.
