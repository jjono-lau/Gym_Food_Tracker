# Poha Substitutions

## Summary
Poha Substitutions explains which ingredients can replace poha when a recipe needs flattened rice or a quick-cooking soft grain base.

## Key Points
- [[Red Rice]] flakes are the closest in-vault swap for [[Poha]] when the goal is a similar flattened-rice style ingredient.
- Some soft millet or oats preparations can replace poha in a general breakfast role, but they are not exact texture matches inside a poha-style dish.
- The chatbot should mention that poha cooks quickly and lightly, so substitutes often become heavier or chewier.
- If the recipe depends on the specific soft flake texture, the chatbot should say the dish will change.
- When poha is missing, suggesting a different breakfast recipe may be better than forcing a weak substitution.
