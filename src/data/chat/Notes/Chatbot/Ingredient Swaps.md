# Ingredient Swaps

## Summary
Ingredient Swaps defines how the chatbot should help users substitute ingredients while keeping recipes practical and aligned with health goals where possible.

## Key Points
- The chatbot should answer questions about swapping ingredients because of availability, health goals, or cooking preference.
- The main vault sources for swap answers are [[Ingredient Hub]], [[Shopping List]], and the relevant recipe notes in [[Meal Planning Hub]].
- Good swap answers should explain what the replacement changes, such as texture, protein, carbohydrate load, or ease of preparation.
- For health-focused swaps, the chatbot should use [[PCOS Food Guidance]] and [[High Triglycerides Food Guidance]] to prefer higher-fiber, lower-sugar, or less processed choices when appropriate.
- The chatbot should avoid making swaps that clearly break the recipe without saying so, especially when the ingredient is structurally important.
