# Tamarind Substitutions

## Summary
Tamarind Substitutions explains which souring ingredients can replace tamarind when a recipe needs acidity but the exact flavor is unavailable.

## Key Points
- [[Raw Mango]] is one of the closest vault ingredients for replacing some of the sour effect of [[Tamarind]] in savory dishes.
- The chatbot should say clearly that the sourness will feel different because tamarind has a deeper, darker flavor than raw mango.
- In some recipes, reducing sour intensity and relying on the other spices is better than forcing a perfect tamarind replacement.
- Tamarind-forward dishes may still work with a substitute, but the result can become a different style of meal.
- The chatbot should be careful not to promise an exact flavor match.
