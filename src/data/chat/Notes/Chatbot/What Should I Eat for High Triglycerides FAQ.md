# What Should I Eat for High Triglycerides FAQ

## Summary
What Should I Eat for High Triglycerides FAQ is a direct-answer note for chatbot questions about food choices that support healthier triglyceride levels.

## Key Points
- A useful answer should emphasize vegetables, legumes, whole grains, lean proteins, less added sugar, and less heavily processed food.
- Sugary drinks, desserts, and refined carbohydrates are often the highest-yield place to improve food quality.
- The chatbot should also mention alcohol caution when the question is specifically about triglycerides.
- The best answer style is a few practical food examples plus a small number of suggested swaps.
- The chatbot should connect the answer to meal ideas or ingredient substitutions inside the vault.
