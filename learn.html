Healthy triglycerides in plain language

Short cards, cute icons, and the science made kind.

💧

What are triglycerides?

They are the fat your body stores after you eat. High levels make blood thicker and can stress the heart.

Aim for steady energy, not sugar spikes.

🥗

Why cut sugar & refined carbs?

They convert quickly into triglycerides. Swap with fibre-rich carbs like millets, veggies, legumes.

Try plate method: 50% veggies, 25% protein, 25% smart carbs.

🚫

Alcohol & fried food

Alcohol is stored as fat first. Deep frying adds trans fats that raise triglycerides.

Keep alcohol rare; choose grilling, steaming, tadka with less oil.

🏃‍♀️

Move daily

Activity clears triglycerides from the blood faster and boosts HDL.

10k steps + 3 strength days is a powerful combo.

Plate plan

Half veggies, quarter protein, quarter smart carbs. Add healthy fat.

Move after meals

8–10 minute walk lowers post-meal triglyceride spike.

Sleep & stress

7–9 hours sleep and breathing breaks keep cravings calmer.
